About Me

Hello! My name is Priya.

I moved to Austin in 2012, and I live with my husband and daughter. My family, husband and daughter mean the world to me and I LOVE them so much!

I have my Bachelors in Information Technology from Anna University, India and I worked as a Software Developer in Infosys Ltd for a while. I really enjoyed working at Infosys, and it was a great place for me to start my career.

I loved being a software developer. It is so much fun to see the small code we do turn in to big applications or cool websites that we get to use in our everyday life. That’s why I couldn’t wait to come back to this industry again
